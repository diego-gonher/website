Diego Gonzalez-Hernandez

Astrophysics PhD candidate at UC Santa Barbara. NASA FINESST recipient. Intern at Lawrence-Berkeley National Lab.

Hello there!

My name is Diego, and I'm a PhD candidate at UC Santa Barbara, where I am part of the ENIGMA group. My work is at the intersection of machine learning, statistics and cosmology. Take a look around my website to learn more about me and my work!

About me

Research

I'm interested in cosmology, statistics and machine learning. My current projects mostly involve enabling and improving constraints on the Epoch of Cosmic Reionization using a combination of statistics and ML algorithms.
