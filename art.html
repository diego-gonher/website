Current Projects

I love spending some of my free time doing working on projects where I get to exercise my artistic expression. Among different things that I've tried, I've been mostly consistent at creating generative art and working on my own music.

Generative Art

Under the name of Blind Restitution, I love to create generative art by using custom Iterative Function Systems (IFS) and some basic editing techniques. You can see all my pieces on my dedicated Instagram page!
